Design System - Components

  • Accela Design System - Components

Design systems play a crucial role in ensuring a consistent and efficient user experience across digital products. When designing a system for Accela, which is known for its software solutions for government agencies, it's important to consider elements that align with the specific needs and requirements of the users in the public sector.

Role

Product Designer / Developer

About

Building a design system for a mobile app for maintaining consistency, improving efficiency, ensuring scalability, fostering collaboration, promoting maintainability, reinforcing branding, enhancing user experience, addressing accessibility, and providing valuable documentation for the development team.

Research for Button Component

Researching for the mobile button component in our design system involves understanding how users interact with buttons on small screens. We explore optimal sizes, colors, and placements to ensure a seamless and intuitive experience. By studying user preferences and behaviors, we aim to create a button that enhances mobile usability and responsiveness.

Final Design

The final design for our mobile button is sleek and user-friendly. It's easy to tap, with rounded edges for a smooth look. Accessibility is at its core, featuring a comfortable size and high contrast to ensure everyone can engage seamlessly..
