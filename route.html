Route Sheet Planning for Accela Mobile-App

  • Homepage
  • Portfolio
  • Route Sheet Planning for Mobile App

Accela Mobile App simplifies government processes, allowing users to manage permits, inspections, and civic services on their mobile devices. It brings efficiency to tasks like inspections and licensing, ensuring a responsive and accessible experience for citizens and officials alike.

Role

Product Designer

About

Inspectors planning their own inspection routes currently face the challenge of logging into Civic Platform to add route sheet orders. Recognizing the need for a more efficient solution, inspectors sought a streamlined process, as the existing method proved cumbersome and time-consuming.

Research and Insights

Offer inspectors a versatile route planning tool that suits different inspection types, team sizes, and durations. Let inspectors optimize routes based on priority and location easily. Create a user-friendly route planning feature that works seamlessly with other tools, making the entire inspection process smoother.

Designs Strategy

To start, I led a workshop to brainstorm and explore various ideas for creating the Route Sheet Planning feature tailored for inspectors.

Designs for User Testing

I created user-friendly designs for testing how inspectors use the Route Sheet Inspection feature in the Accela Mobile App. These designs focus on making it easy and efficient for inspectors to plan their routes and complete inspections smoothly.

Learnings from Usability Testing

Final Design

Inspectors can now plan their inspection routes efficiently using the map-based feature, saving time and effort previously spent on manual logins to Civic Platform. The dynamic map interface allowed inspectors to make real-time adjustments to their routes, ensuring flexibility and adaptability based on changing inspection requirements. By providing inspectors with a user-friendly tool for route planning, the project contributed to improved productivity, allowing inspectors to focus more on their core responsibilities.
