  • AI-POC for Mobile App

Accela specializes in modernizing government services through innovative software solutions. They streamline processes, enabling efficient civic management and better citizen engagement. Accela's platforms digitize permits, inspections, and licensing for more accessible and responsive governance. Accela Mobile App simplifies government processes, allowing users to manage permits, inspections, and civic services on their mobile devices. It brings efficiency to tasks like inspections and licensing, ensuring a responsive and accessible experience for citizens and officials alike.

Role

Product Designer

About

In response to the growing demand for efficiency and accuracy in inspections, our team embarked on a Proof of Concept (POC) project to integrate Artificial Intelligence (AI) capabilities into the existing Accela Mobile App.The goal was to enable Inspectors to initiate and complete restaurant inspections using voice commands or minimal screen interaction, ideal for situations when their hands are occupied. Implemented Natural Language Processing (NLP) to generate inspection reports seamlessly.

Research and Insights

As an inspector, I require the capability to initiate and finalize a restaurant inspection seamlessly on my tablet, utilizing voice commands or minimal screen interaction due to busy hands. The integration of Natural Language Processing (NLP) is crucial for generating inspection reports. Additionally, the ability to pause during inspections without losing recorded observations is essential for efficient workflow.
